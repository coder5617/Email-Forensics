Email Header Analyzer

Analyze email headers to check authentication, track delivery paths, and identify potential security issues.



How to get email headers:

  • Gmail: Open email ‚Üí More (3 dots) ‚Üí Show original
  • Outlook: Open email ‚Üí File ‚Üí Properties ‚Üí Internet headers
  • Yahoo: Open email ‚Üí More ‚Üí View raw message
  • Apple Mail: View ‚Üí Message ‚Üí All Headers
